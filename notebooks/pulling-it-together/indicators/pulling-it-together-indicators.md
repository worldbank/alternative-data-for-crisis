# Pulling it Together - Indicators
## Summary
This chapter of the course focuses on summarizing the analysis developed in previous classes into indicators to provide quantitative information about the crisis extent at different geography levels. Generating these type of indicators help users to organize the information and get a broader picture of the situation. This class presents two type of indicators: Households and Communities and Industry and Commerce.

## Learning Objectives
The main goal of this class is to teach students to summarize different types of analysis into meaninful quantitative indicators for Households and Communities and Industry and Commerce.
